Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our treatments

We treat a wide range of conditions including back pain, neck pain, sciatica, disc herniation, sports injuries, arthritis, frozen shoulder, plantar fasciitis, and post-surgical rehabilitation. Our expertise spans physiotherapy, chiropractic care, and manual therapy techniques.

A typical treatment session lasts 30-45 minutes, depending on your condition and treatment plan. Your first consultation may take longer (about 60 minutes) as we conduct a thorough assessment and create a personalized treatment strategy.

No, you don't need a referral. You can directly book an appointment with us. However, if you have medical reports or imaging studies (X-rays, MRI), please bring them along as they help us understand your condition better.

The number of sessions varies based on your condition, severity, and response to treatment. Acute conditions may improve in 3-6 sessions, while chronic issues might require 8-12 sessions. We'll provide a clear treatment plan after your initial assessment.

Our treatments are generally comfortable and pain-free. Some techniques like deep tissue work or spinal adjustments may cause mild discomfort initially, but this is temporary. We always work within your comfort level and adjust techniques accordingly.
