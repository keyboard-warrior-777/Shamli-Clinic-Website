20+ Years Experience

Dr. Jitendra Kumar Singh

Founder & Senior Physiotherapist/Chiropractor

Dr. Jitendra Kumar Singh is a pioneering figure in physiotherapy and chiropractic care in the Shamli region. With over two decades of clinical experience, he has become renowned for his exceptional diagnostic skills and comprehensive treatment approaches. His journey began with a passion for understanding human biomechanics and the body's innate healing capabilities, which led him to pursue advanced certifications in both physiotherapy and chiropractic medicine.

Throughout his career, Dr. Singh has treated thousands of patients, from acute sports injuries to chronic degenerative conditions. His expertise spans complex spinal disorders, neurological rehabilitation, and orthopedic conditions. He is particularly known for his success in treating disc herniations, sciatica, and sports-related injuries without surgery or medications.

Beyond his clinical practice, Dr. Singh is passionate about patient education. As co-creator of the "Physio Couple" YouTube channel, he shares valuable health information with a wider audience, helping people understand their conditions and learn self-care techniques. His commitment to continuous learning keeps him at the forefront of modern therapeutic techniques.

Dr. Amrita Rani

Co-Founder & Senior Physiotherapist/Chiropractor

Dr. Amrita Rani is a highly respected physiotherapist and chiropractor with over 20 years of dedicated service to patient care. Her approach combines clinical excellence with a deep understanding of the emotional and psychological aspects of healing. Known for her gentle yet effective treatment techniques, Dr. Rani has helped countless patients overcome pain and regain their quality of life.

Dr. Rani's expertise lies in treating complex musculoskeletal conditions, women's health issues, and geriatric rehabilitation. She has particular success with frozen shoulder, arthritis management, and neurological conditions like Bell's palsy. Her holistic perspective ensures that every patient receives care tailored not just to their physical symptoms but to their overall well-being.

As co-creator of the "Physio Couple" YouTube channel alongside Dr. Singh, Dr. Rani is committed to making healthcare knowledge accessible to everyone. Her educational videos on exercise techniques, pain management, and preventive care have helped thousands of people take charge of their health. Her warm, patient-centered approach makes even the most anxious patients feel comfortable and confident in their treatment.
